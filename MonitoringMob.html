Frequency
This map provides you with a visualization of the frequency of trips generated by each public transportation mode. We can observe the highest and lowest transportation modes and stations in terms of trips frequency.
Accessibility
An efficient public transportation system is based on a good distribution of well-connected stations in a city. This map allows us to see all the different stations in Red Town and the radius each one serves.

Users per Hour

This chart displays the number of users per hour in real-time. We are able to notice the most commonly used transportation system as well as the peak hours. By a comparison of the days, months and years we are also able to observe the weekly dynamics and seasonal patterns.
